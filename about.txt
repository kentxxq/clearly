Clearly is a robust and user-friendly browser extension dedicated to maintaining your online privacy and keeping your browsing history tidy. 

It empowers you with sophisticated search capabilities for precise manual history management, while also featuring a powerful automation engine. 

You can effortlessly create custom rules to automatically remove history records based on specific domains or keywords. 

With a built-in scheduler that initiates cleaning every minute and a detailed activity log for transparency, Clearly ensures your browsing data is handled exactly the way you want it. 

We are committed to continuous improvement and will regularly release updates with new features and optimizations to enhance your experience.

Experience a cleaner, faster, and more private web with Clearly.
